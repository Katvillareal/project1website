    • City Safety CCTV Cameras

    The Wellington City Council manages the closed circuit televicon (CCTV) surveillance cameras in central Wellington which are monitored by volunteers and the police.

    This is used to make Wellington a safe city and target and prevent crime or mischievous actions, this also helps to increase public safety.

    Volunteers at the camera base record data and observe any incidents or situations that may occur. All cameras are marked with a sign that says 'Crime prevention and safety cameras are operating in this area'.

    Chorus Fibre installation

    Fibre cabel is installed in your street to your property. Fibre cable run to the external termination point (ETP) which is attached to the side of the building.

    The ETP is the box attached to the outside of your property where the external fibre cable from your street connects to your internal wiring.

    Fibre is the best broadband available with Chorus, delivering fast speed. The faster the connection, the faster data moves through it meaning you can do more in a shorter space of time. Both copper (which is traditionally used to get online) and fibre provide a dedicated fixed line to the house. Copper transmits data via electrical signal while fibre sends data as pulses of light.

    Parking Sensor

    Parking sensors are a small electronic device that is installed in the ground of each parking spot. It detects and records the time a vehicle arrives and leaves the parking spot.

    Wellington has introduced around 3500 parking censors in the metered parking spaces in Central Wellington.

    The sensors are very accurate and record the exact time a vehicle enters and leaves the parking spot. The sensors are synchronized through a central server, which is set up NZ time. The devices parking wardens use are synchronized with the same server.

    Power

    Underground power cables is the replacement of overhead cables providing high-voltage electrical power or telecommunications, with underground cables.

    Wellington has an extensive network of underground electricity and telecommunications cabling that provide important service to residents, businesses and organizations.
